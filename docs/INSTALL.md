# How to put Smoothie! on the iPad

For Elana. Short version: open in Safari, tap Share, tap Add to Home Screen, then launch from the home-screen icon (not Safari).

## Steps

1. **Open Safari on the iPad.** Not Chrome, not the Google app. Safari specifically. PWAs only install properly from Safari on iOS.
2. **Go to the URL.** Will will text you the link the first time. Once installed you won't need it again.
3. **Wait for it to fully load.** You should see the "Ποιος παίζει;" (Who's playing?) screen with two cartoon faces, Ezra and Άρης.
4. **Tap the Share button.** Bottom of the screen on iPhone, top-right on iPad. It's the square with the up-arrow.
5. **Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen."** Confirm with "Add" in the top-right.
6. **Now close Safari** and open Smoothie! from its new home-screen icon. This step matters: the audio works much more reliably when launched from the home screen than from a Safari tab.

## First-time setup

- Have the iPad volume up. The game speaks Greek phrases out loud.
- On the very first tap (pick Ezra or Άρης), iOS will warm up the audio engine. The first phrase plays after that.
- If you don't hear anything: check the silent-mode switch on the side of the iPad. The mute switch silences the game.

## Who picks which mode

- **Ezra** sees all 4 levels and can pick any of them.
- **Άρης** only sees Levels 1 and 2. Levels 3 and 4 are visible but grayed out ("για μεγάλους" / for big kids). This is intentional so he doesn't faceplant on phrases he can't parse.
- The kid can switch profiles at any time with the small face-button top-right of the home screen.

## What each level does

- **Level 1, "Βάλε…":** Dad asks "Put X in the blender!" Kid taps the fruit. Tiles stay hidden until the phrase plays so the kid has to actually listen.
- **Level 2, "Θέλω…":** Different carrier phrases ("I want X" / "Mom wants X" / "Dad wants X" / "Give me X"). Same fruit, different sentence shapes. Sometimes a silly sock/flip-flop/toothbrush appears as a distractor. Dad does a funny "ΌΧΙ!" face and there's no penalty.
- **Level 3, "…και…":** Two fruits ("Mom wants banana and strawberry!"). Kid taps both.
- **Level 4, "μεγάλο…":** Size and quantity ("big watermelon" / "small grape" / "lots of apples"). The choice tiles show the same fruit at different sizes; the kid has to use the modifier word to pick.

## What to do if it stops working

- **No audio at all:** force-quit the app (swipe up from the dock, swipe Smoothie! up), then reopen. The audio engine sometimes needs a fresh start after the iPad sleeps.
- **The game looks wrong / blank screen:** force-quit, then reopen. If still bad, delete the icon and reinstall from the URL.
- **"Tap to start" loop:** Will to debug. The IndexedDB profile might be corrupt.
- **Audio sounds robotic:** That's the placeholder voice (iOS Melina TTS). Will is going to record his own voice for the phrases later. Until then, robot-Greek is the cost of business.
